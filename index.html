Mock Location

Get it on Google Play
// Features

Everything you need to own your location

📍
Map Teleportation

Instantly set your device's GPS to any coordinates worldwide — ideal for testing geo-restricted features, simulating users in different regions, or simply keeping your real location private.

🕹️
Joystick Movement

360° directional control with 5 speed tiers — Walk (1.5 m/s), Jog, City, Highway, and Ludicrous (60 m/s). Variable strength based on joystick position.

⏺️
Route Recording

Capture and replay real-world GPS traces for thorough location testing across different path scenarios. Road snapping aligns recorded routes perfectly to road networks via OSRM.

⏯️
Route Playback

Replay at 0.25x–4x speed, forward or reverse, with seek scrubbing. Sub-step interpolation at 20 fps ensures silky smooth movement.

📋
Playlist System

Chain multiple routes into a sequence with drag-and-drop reordering, per-item loop counts, and instant or interpolated transitions.

🛠️
GPS Realism

Simulate real-world GPS conditions including signal drift and accuracy variance — essential for robust location app testing. Full GPX 1.1 import and export support.

Obie Studios is an independent Android app studio focused on building tools that are genuinely useful — not bloated, not half-finished, not ad-riddled garbage. Just software that works the way you'd expect it to.

GPS Simulator is our first app. It started as a developer's need — a reliable, precise mock location tool for testing location-based features without physically traveling. It grew into something used by developers, privacy-focused users, and professionals who need accurate control over their device's GPS output. More apps are in development, all held to the same standard: precision, polish, and respect for the user.
